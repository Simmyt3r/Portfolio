The why behind the work

I'm a software engineer and entrepreneur based in Abuja, Nigeria. As Managing Director of Simeon's Laboratories & Co Technologies Ltd (Silabs), I lead a multi-disciplinary team building software solutions, training programs, and digital platforms that create real opportunities for Nigerians.

My journey started at De-East Computers in 2012, where I spent four years learning computer fundamentals from the ground up. That hands-on foundation shaped my philosophy: technology only matters when it actually works for people.

Beyond code, I serve as Acting Director of Media & PR for Sanitatem Rehabilitation International Initiative (SRII), mentor aspiring developers, and run workshops for student entrepreneurs. I believe African tech can solve African problems at scale — and I'm building toward that vision.
