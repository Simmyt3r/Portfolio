about me

Engineer. Founder.
Community Builder.

I'm a software engineer and entrepreneur from Nigeria with a strong foundation in full-stack web development, systems design, and applied AI. As Managing Director of Simeon's Laboratories & Co Technologies Ltd (Silabs), I lead a team of 9 directors building software platforms, training programs, and digital services that create real economic opportunity.

My journey started at De-East Computers in 2012 — four years learning computer fundamentals from the ground up before managing the business. That hands-on beginning shaped my approach: I build things that actually work, for people with real constraints, in real environments.

Beyond code, I serve as Acting Director of Media & PR for Sanitatem Rehabilitation International Initiative (SRII), mentor aspiring developers, and co-founded UniXpress — an academic platform serving Nigerian university students. My vision is a Nigeria where technology is built locally, taught locally, and solves local problems at scale.
