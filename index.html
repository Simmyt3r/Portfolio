The person behind the work

I'm a software engineer and entrepreneur based in Abuja, Nigeria. As Managing Director of Simeon's Laboratories & Co Technologies Ltd (Silabs), I lead a team building software solutions, training programs, and digital platforms that create real opportunities for Nigerians.

My journey started at De-East Computers in 2012 — four years of hands-on hardware and systems work before I ever wrote a line of professional code. That grounding shaped my philosophy: technology only matters when it works for real people.

I graduated from Nigerian Army University Biu with a B.Sc. in Software Engineering (CGPA: 4.46/5.00) while simultaneously running a registered company, freelancing, teaching, and serving as Ag. Director of Media & PR at a rehabilitation NGO. I also built Techdialect — a STEM learning platform in Nigerian local languages — because the language barrier in African tech is a problem nobody talks about enough.
